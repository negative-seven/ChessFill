How to play:
  • You control all the pieces at any time. You don't need to alternate between white and black.
  • Pieces move and capture like they do in chess, with some exceptions:
    • Pawns can move in all 4 cardinal directions and capture in all 4 diagonals, but never move by 2 tiles.
    • There is no check/checkmate; kings are not restricted by check rules.
  • Each piece can only move once.
  • After a piece is moved, a new piece will randomly spawn.
  • Aim for as many moves as you can!
